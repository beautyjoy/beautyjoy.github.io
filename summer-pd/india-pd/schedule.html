Schedule

The BJC Team has created a tentative schedule for the benefit of PD 2019 participants:

  • Monday
    • (Morning Session)
      • Introductions of instructors and all participants through icebreakers
      • Short presentation of BJC and CSforALL
      • CSPathshala Introduction
      • Introduction to Snap! and creating Snap! accounts
      • Divide into teams for the Snap! Project.
    • (Afternoon Session)
      • Overview of BJC middle-school vs BJC high school curriculum
      • Work through short examples from BJC in pairs
      • CS Unplugged Activities
  • Tuesday
    • (Morning Session)
      • Introduction through Computing in News
      • Snap! Concepts - Lists with methods of map/keep/combine
      • Snap! Concepts - Higher Order Functions & Using functions as data
    • (Afternoon Session)
      • Snap! Concepts - Iterative Methods & Constructs
      • How to build our own higher-order functions?
      • CS Unplugged Activities
  • Wednesday
    • (Morning Session)
      • Recap of concepts from Day 2
      • Some more insights through Computing in News
      • CS Unplugged Activities
    • (Afternoon Session)
      • Introduction to Recursion in Snap!
      • Talking about the libraries which can be used in Snap!
  • Thursday
    • (Morning Session)
      • Plezmo Introduction and demos which would be carried out by the Plezmo team.
    • (Afternoon Session)
      • Plezmo Demos continued
      • Currently, Snap! And Plezmo Integration is in progress. Prof. Garcia will share an update on the same.
  • Friday
    • (Morning Session)
      • Beginning the day with Computing in News
      • Presentation of Team Projects
      • Elaborate Discussion on social implications of Computing and how to introduce a similar discussion in classrooms amongst students.
    • (Afternoon Session)
      • Discussion on how BJC Curriculum will be incorporated by different teachers in their school
      • Sharing of BJC Resources, Surveys, Photos and Farewell
