The Mission: ESL for Silicon Valley's immigrant population

Silicon Valley (Santa Clara and San Mateo counties) is currently home to over 175 thousand Spanish-speaking adults who identify their English language skills as insufficient for full participation in their greater community. However, the current supply of high-quality educational programs for adult English language learners (ELLs) in Silicon Valley is limited to serving only five to seven thousand learners, irrespective of their first language. Our mission is to close this drastic gap between the huge demand and limited supply of quality ESL instruction in Silicon Valley.
